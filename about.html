About me

Hello! I'm Aakash Kumar Mathur, a dedicated web developer with a passion for crafting interactive and engaging digital experiences. With a background in Information Technology, I have honed my skills in web development, focusing on both the aesthetic and functional aspects of websites.

My Journey

Looking back at my 10th grade, I see it as a pivotal year that shaped my academic journey and laid the foundation for my future in engineering. It was a time of significant growth, both personally and academically, where I began to discover my passion for problem-solving and technology.

The 12th grade was a transformative year for me, one that solidified my interest in technology and set me on the path to becoming an engineer. Among the various subjects I studied, Python programming stood out as a pivotal experience that not only deepened my understanding of coding but also fueled my passion for problem-solving and innovation.

Till my 3rd year of college, I expanded my technical skills by diving into multiple programming languages and web technologies, including Java, C++, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Through hands-on projects, I strengthened my understanding of object-oriented programming with Java and C++, while also exploring the world of web development by creating interactive and visually appealing websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This year has been pivotal in shaping my coding abilities and preparing me for more advanced challenges in the tech field.
